I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH), under the supervision of Dr. Jyothi Vedurada. My current research interests include program analysis and compiler optimization using large language models and static analysis.

I am a Senior Lecturer at the Dept. of ICT, Faculty of Technology, South Eastern University of Sri Lanka (currently on study leave). I completed my MSc in Computer Science from the Dept. of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Moratuwa, and my B.Sc. (Hons) Specialised in Computer Science from South Eastern University of Sri Lanka.
